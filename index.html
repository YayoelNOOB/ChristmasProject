Introduction

Today, I will show you some Christmas Easter eggs that we can find in video games. Some of these Easter eggs only appear in December. There are many more; however, I believe these are classic games that many readers may have played in their childhood.

Best Easter Eggs

GTA San Andreas

In Grand Theft Auto: San Andreas, if you play during the holiday season (usually around December), you'll experience a transformation in the game world with snow covering the streets of Los Santos. This not only has a visual impact but also affects gameplay, as vehicles become more slippery.

World of Warcraft

WoW usually has holiday events, and Winter Veil is its Christmas celebration. You can find gifts in various locations in the game and participate in festive-themed quests. There's even a special boss named "Greatfather Winter" that you can face during this event.

Hitman

In the game Hitman, specifically in the "Showstopper" mission in Paris, there's an event called "Holiday Hoarders." During this event, the level transforms into a Christmas party, and your objective is to stop thieves who are stealing Christmas presents.

Overwatch

Overwatch celebrates Winter Wonderland, an annual event that transforms many game maps with Christmas decorations. Additionally, characters get winter-themed skins. This event usually includes special game modes and loot boxes with festive items.

Batman: Arkham Origins

In Batman: Arkham Origins, there's a showdown with the Joker that takes place on Christmas Eve. Gotham City is covered in snow, and the Joker has a unique festive appearance. This moment is memorable for fans of the game.
